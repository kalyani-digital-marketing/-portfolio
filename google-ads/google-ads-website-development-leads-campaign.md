# Website Development Lead Generation Campaign

## Project Overview

### Objective
Generate qualified leads for website development services through Google Search Ads.

### Campaign Type
Google Search Campaign

### Industry
Website Development & Web Design Services

### Campaign Period
9 Jan 2026 – 5 Jun 2026

---

## Target Audience

Businesses looking for:

- Website Development
- Website Design
- E-commerce Website Development
- Shopify Website Development
- Business Website Development

---

## Strategy

### Keyword Targeting

Examples:

- website development company
- web development services
- website developers near me
- website design company
- ecommerce website development

### Ad Assets

- Sitelinks
- Callouts
- Structured Snippets

### Optimization Activities

- Search Term Analysis
- Negative Keyword Addition
- Ad Copy Testing
- Bid Optimization
- Device Performance Monitoring

- Search campaigns generated targeted traffic.
- High-intent keywords produced better engagement.

- Structured snippets and ad extensions can further improve CTR.
